THE BEST MOVIES ACCORDING TO ME

MY TOP 3 MOVIES

GEETA GOVINDAM

Genre: Romance, Comedy, Emotional

Why I Love This Movie:
I absolutely love this movie because of its crisp and heartwarming love story between the male and female leads. They meet randomly, and due to a sudden brake by the bus driver, the male lead accidentally falls onto the sleeping female lead, resulting in an unintentional kiss.

Later, they unexpectedly meet again in a village, where the plot takes a surprising turn — the male lead’s sister is set to marry the female lead’s elder brother, who holds a highly respected status in their community.

Despite their awkward past encounter, both try to hide the situation to avoid ruining their siblings' joyful wedding.

This film is one of the most fantastic movies I’ve ever watched.

DEAR COMRADE

Why I Love This Movie:
In this film, the male and female leads are the same as in the previous one, but the story takes a more intense and emotional turn.

It features powerful action and follows the male lead’s incredible journey of character development — from being a hot-headed thug to becoming a calm, nature-loving, and caring individual. His transformation is inspiring.

However, when he returns, he discovers a heartbreaking truth: the female lead is in a coma after being assaulted and humiliated by her cricket coach for refusing his inappropriate advances.

This emotional and gripping storyline makes the movie deeply impactful and unforgettable.

MAN OF STEEL(SUPERMAN)

Why I Love This Movie:
I don't even have the words to fully express how much I love this movie — it's an absolute masterpiece.

From its storytelling to the emotional weight and powerful performances, everything about it left a lasting impression on me.
It's one of those films that stays with you long after the credits roll.
